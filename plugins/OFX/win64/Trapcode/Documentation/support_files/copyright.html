About Trapcode

Trapcode makes software for professional visual effects and motion graphic design software that can be seen on television shows, commercials, music video and feature films. Trapcode is a privately-held Aktiebolag (AB) based in Sweden and is founded and operated by Peder Norrby.

About Red Giant Software

Red Giant Software offers a vast range of visual effects plug-ins, with over 30 packages designed for professional users and consumers. The product line features industry-leading effects for designing realistic fire, rain, and water; adding popular film and movie looks; and creating perfect keys. Red Giant effects have enhanced feature films such as The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, Mummy 3, and Grindhouse, and add sparkle to cable and broadcast content from NBC Universal, Food Network, ESPN, Disney, CNN, Comedy Central, MTV, and TNT.
